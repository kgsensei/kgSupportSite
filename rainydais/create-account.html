RainyDais Create Account

A short guide that will show you how to make an account on RainyDais.

[Return To Home]

Step 1

Go to https://RainyDais.com and navigate to the link that is labeled "Signup".

Step 2

Fill out all of the required form details.
You can change your username later if you want.

Be sure to remember your password, you will need it to login later.

Check the "Agree Terms" checkbox and then click "Register".

Step 3

Once you click register it should create your account and redirect you to a login screen, here you will need to remember your username and password so you can login and start using RainyDais!
