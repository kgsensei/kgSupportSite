RainyDais Get Mobile App

A short guide that will show you how to get the RainyDais mobile app on Android.

[Return To Home] Currently iOS Devices are not Supported

Step 1

Navigate to the Google Play Store app on your Android device.

Step 2

Search for an app called "RainyDais" (without the quotes). It won't show up if you put a space in the search term to make sure it's all one word.

After making the search a few app results should appear. Click on the one called RainyDais with the publisher being kgsensei.

Step 3

After you get to the application page all you need to do is click install. Please note that you will need to sign in to use the app.
