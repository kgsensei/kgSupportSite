RainyDais Enable Embeds

A short guide that will show you how to enable embeds on RainyDais.

[Return To Home]

Step 1

Go to your RainyDais Settings. In order to do this you will need to be signed in to an account.

Step 2

Check the boxes that say "YouTube Embeds" and "Spotify Embeds". It will automatically save. Then all you need to do is go to the feed again.
