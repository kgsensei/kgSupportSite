RainyDais Reset Password

A short guide that will show you how to reset your password on RainyDais.

[Return To Home]

Step 1

Go to the reset password page and enter the email you signed up with.

Step 2

Fill in the email prompt and then submit the form. This will send you an email with your account password reset link. You can use this link to reset your password. Keep in mind that this link will expire anywhere from 15 to 30 minutes after generation.
