Anonymous Ext. Enable/Disable

In this guide you will learn how to enable or disable the Anonymous Extension.

[Return To Home]

Edge

Go to the extensions management page by typing edge://extensions in the search bar.
Scroll to the Anonmyous Extension entry. There will be a slider where you can turn it on and off.

Chrome

Go to the extensions management page by typing chrome://extensions in the search bar.
Scroll to the Anonmyous Extension entry. There will be a slider where you can turn it on and off.
